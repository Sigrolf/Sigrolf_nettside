Sigurd Rolfsnes

Gymnast and physics/mathematics student at NTNU

I value discipline, body control, and long‑term effort. Years of gymnastics taught me patience and precision — the kind of quiet practice that builds lasting strength.

I study physics and mathematics at NTNU because I’m curious about how things work at a fundamental level. I enjoy combining physical challenges with analytical thinking and problem solving.

I’m motivated by learning and improvement. Whether it’s refining a movement, understanding a concept, or creating something simple and clean, I try to keep my approach focused, calm, and honest.
