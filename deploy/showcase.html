An astro story

Nightfall Odyssey

A slow walk from the first glow on the horizon to the quiet shimmer of deep space.

Chapter 1 · First Light

Blue hour launch

We set up under a fading cobalt sky, letting the last daylight guide composition while the air cools and the first stars blink on.

Chapter 2 · Traverse

Into the dust lanes

The Milky Way arches overhead; each exposure reveals more structure—dark nebulae carving rivers through the galactic core.

Chapter 3 · Encounter

Starlight close-up

The Pleiades emerge as a tight cluster; reflection nebulae glow icy blue against the void, a quiet counterpoint to the galactic sprawl.

Chapter 4 · Return

Andromeda on the horizon

A final long exposure catches Andromeda rising, a faint smudge to the eye but sprawling in the sensor—our reminder of how close the night can feel.
