About Me

{% if about_portrait_url != '' %} {% endif %}

Hi, I'm Sigurd Rolfsnes from Trondheim, Norway. I love exploring the night sky and capturing its wonders through my lens. My work is inspired by the beauty of the cosmos and the landscapes of Norway.

Artist Statement: Photography is my way of connecting with the universe, sharing the awe and serenity I find in the night sky and wild places.

Milestones

  • 2025 - Launched this portfolio website
  • 2024 - Solo exhibition in Trondheim
  • 2023 - Published in National Geographic Norway
  • 2022 - First aurora photo featured on NASA APOD
