Sigurd Rolfsnes

Astro and landscape photographer from Trondheim, Norway

I create images where the night sky and wild places meet — translating quiet moments into light, color, and texture. My work draws from the awe of the cosmos and the rugged beauty of Norway.

When not under stars, I’m exploring coastlines and mountains, chasing atmospheres and stories. Thanks for visiting — I hope these images inspire curiosity and calm.
