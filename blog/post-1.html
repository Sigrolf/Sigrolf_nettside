Chasing the Aurora

June 20, 2025 Aurora Night Norway

A night under the northern lights in Norway, with tips for capturing auroras.

On June 20th, 2025, I set out to capture the elusive aurora borealis above the Norwegian landscape. The night was clear, the air crisp, and the anticipation high as I waited for the first green ribbons to appear in the sky.

Preparation & Gear

For aurora photography, I recommend a fast wide-angle lens (f/2.8 or faster), a sturdy tripod, and a camera with good high ISO performance. Dress warmly and bring extra batteries!

Camera Settings

  • ISO: 1600-3200
  • Aperture: f/2.8
  • Shutter: 5-15 seconds
  • Manual focus to infinity

Tips for Success

  • Scout your location in daylight
  • Use a remote shutter or timer
  • Watch the aurora forecast
  • Experiment with foregrounds

That night, the aurora danced for hours, painting the sky in shades of green and purple. I left with a memory card full of images and a heart full of wonder.
